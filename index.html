Who is
Tania Shakira Kankindi?

I, Tania Shakirah Kankindi was born to a Ugandan family of Rwandan origin on 29th of November 1994. I attended the Multitech Business School where I graduated with a Bachelor’s in Mass Communication and Journalism. I attended Mariam Ndagire Film and Performing Arts Center where i got a Diploma in acting before working for 3 years as the Public Relations Manager for Ykee Benda, a renowned Ugandan musician.

Am an actor with a self-driven motivation to reach greater heights! Been on different sets, acted in various TV-shows, stage plays, MTN-adverts and looking forward to many more commercial adverts as well!

Nominations/Accolades

I nominated for the best actress in a feature film for “Invincible Cuffs” at Uganda’s biggest movie awards (Uganda Film Festival) in 2016.

She received another nomination and won Best Actress In A Supporting Role in Pearl Film Festival of the year 2021 for her performance in “My Husband’s Wife” directed by Mariam Ndagire.
